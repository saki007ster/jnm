Purpose

JustNamkeens is an online store for getting the authentic local market namkeens from across the country.

Our main purpose is to solve the immediate needs of all the individuals settled in metros craving for their hometown flavours and waiting for someone to get it from there.We would like to connect people with their hometowns and make them feel at home away from home.

Our Business Philosophy is to the get the best flavours of the local vendors to your tastebud and serve it with full zeal.

We would like to reach every nook and corner of the country and spread the local vendors name across the country to get more fulfilment and satisfaction. Our goal is to become one stop shop for all your Namkeen needs with a loyal customer following.
